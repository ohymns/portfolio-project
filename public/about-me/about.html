Photographer and aspiring Interaction Designer


I'm Olivia, a 20-year-old photographer and designer from Oslo, Norway,
now making my mark in Berlin, Germany.
Since I could first hold a camera, my lens has been drawn to human interactions and fleeting moments,
capturing stories in every frame.

With a media and communication degree and A-levels in Photography and Media Studies,
Olivia's academic journey laid the foundation for my artistic pursuits.
Following a year of photography seminars, she showcased my work at Henie Onstad Art Centre,
solidifying my presence in the art world.
Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Interaction Design at Code University,
Olivia merges my passion for photography with innovative design principles,
exploring the intersection of technology and human connection. Eager for new adventures and challenges in photography, videography, and design,
Olivia's creativity knows no bounds.
Each click of my shutter invites you to join my on my journey
of discovery through the captivating landscapes
of human emotion and interaction.
